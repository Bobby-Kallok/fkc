Five Dollar Yacht Club

Exploring themes of belonging in a community that at times can feel like the most exclusive club in the world, Forever King Clark set out to record Five Dollar Yacht Club at DIMM Studios in Freehold, NJ during March of 2025. The EP features Dan Baumgarten on keys and helps expand the FKC sound to new horizons.
