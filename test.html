We're a band. Thanks for coming.

Forever King Clark is an indie rock band from Asbury Park,NJ making warbly coastal music.

Since 2023 they have been active around Asbury Park, Brooklyn, and Washington D.C and elsewhere making music to wiggle to. Their lineup includes primary singer-songwriter and guitarist Bobby Kallok, drummer John-Michael Monroy, and bassist Amy Evans.


about

Forever King Clark started life as a book inside a dream of Bobby Kallok. Since then his mission has been to recall the contents of that book through the band with their brand of...

warbly coastal indie rock


Since then the band has released their first EP, "Five Dollar Yacht Club", played shows around Asbury Park, Brooklyn, and Washington D.C. and has had an all around good time.
