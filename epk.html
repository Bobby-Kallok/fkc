Bio

We are an indie rock band called Forever King Clark from Asbury Park, NJ. As a band we believe in doing as much as possible by ourselves or with others from our area, which is why we have forgone the cookie website and built something ourselves.

Forming in 2023 our lineup consists of singer-songwriter Bobby Kallok, John-Michael on backup vocals and drums,and Amy Evans on bass.

2024 was a busy year for us playing shows in Asbury Park, Brooklyn, and the rest of the shore while also releasing our first three singles. Also during that time we participated in several events for local music labels including contributing a song to Shore Point's Record's "Local Summer" comp and performing at Telegraph Hill Record's What A Wonderful Year music fest. This year we also made our first radio appearance as we performed at WMCX's The X Fest.

Our influences include Mac DeMarco, Weezer, and the Beatles as well as countless other surf rock, indie rock, and garage rock bands from the 60s to present day.
